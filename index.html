    DR. VINH TRAN, DDS

    DENTIST

    Dr.Tran has been improving smiles in the greater Atlanta area for years. His gentle touch abd friendly demeanor allow his patients to truly RELAX during their treatment. Years of advanced education and experience qualify him to guide you to the very BEST decision for your oral health.

    Dr.Tran is devoted to his patients, treating each and everyone with the utmost compassion and respect. His kindness, sense of humor, commitment to personal care, and gentle approach to dentistry keep his patients smiling.

    He was born in Vietnam and raised in Switzerland. He therefore has a multicultural background and comes equipped with several languages in which he is fluent in.

    Dr.Tran is an active member of the American Dental Association and Georgia Dental Association. Dr.Tran and his staff enjoy attending dental conferences,seminars,in order to stock themselves with the most current knowledge and expertise of dental technology and techniques. He loves every aspect of dentistry and is thrilled to be able to offer his high quality care to his patients.

    He is married to his beautiful wife Katrina. He is a passionate skier, enjoys playing music, tennis, table tennis, and especially loves spending time with his family.
